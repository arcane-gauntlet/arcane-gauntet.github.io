Full List

This is a list of every Pokémon in the current National Dex. Each entry links to their individual page. Mega Evolutions and form changes - i.e. Rotom, Oricorio, Lycanroc - are not on this page, but regional forms are.

Boldness denotes that the Pokémon's type was changed for Arcane Gauntlet. These changed types are also underlined on the individual Pokémon's page.

This table is not fully complete yet; we are hard at work filling in the gaps and will be making pages for the individual Pokémon as they are rebalanced. Also, if an ability is marked "tentative" on an individual Pokémon's page, it will not be mentioned in the abilities tab because it isn't finalised (probably because it might be too good).
