Pokémon Arcane Gauntlet

A total retelling!​

Pokémon: Arcane Gauntlet is an upcoming fangame that aims to re-imagine and improve the experience of the Kalos region games, Pokémon X and Y, through increased difficulty, enhanced story, and updated battle mechanics, including having the whole National Pokédex through to Scarlet and Violet (pre-DLC) available!

In addition, a goal of this game is to provide buffs and overhauls to the many Pokémon who have been done dirty by unfortunate stat distributions, shallow movepools, or abilities that hold a Pokémon back from viability! Every Pokémon is someone's favorite - so all of them should be usable!
